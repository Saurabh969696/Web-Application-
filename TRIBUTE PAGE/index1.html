Shivaji Maharaj

1630 - 1680

"Self-discipline and self-control can only lead to good order and morality."

Biography

Shivaji Maharaj was the founder of the Maratha Empire in western India in 1674. He is remembered for his valor, military tactics, and administrative skills.

He carved out an enclave from the declining Adilshahi sultanate of Bijapur. He established a progressive civil rule with well-structured administrative organizations. He was a pioneer in promoting the use of the Marathi language in court and administration.
